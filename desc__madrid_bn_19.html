History: Corodliani thought it to be a copy of a MS recorded at the catalogue (from 1047)of monastery of Ripoll. Hypothesis for both Spanish - Ripoll and Italian - Monte Cassino origin - Meyvaert proved that it was copied from a Beneventan exemplar - based on palaeographical features (pp. 40-42).
