Rouen, Bibliothèque municipale, Ms 26 (olim: A 292)

Astronomical-computistical collection

Parchment — — s. IX3/4(853-867)

Fol. 200 originally used to be after f. 197.

History: Saint-Pierre de Jumieges. The monastery was ravaged by the Vikings between 841 and 852, the monks had to flee carrying with them their precious possessions including books; returned in the half of 10th c. called by duke Guillaume I Longue-Epee, perhaps did not return the books. Six volumes datable to the 9th century are from uncertain origin, including the present Rouen 26 (A. 292).
