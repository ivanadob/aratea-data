Isidore, De natura rerum; De signis caeli; Carmen Sisebuti

Aratea text: De signis caeli

Parchment — 2 Bifolia fols. — 310 × 220 mm — France — s. IX or s. X

Collation: Modern foliation in the upper outher corner of each recto. Probably the two bifolia from the middle of one quire.

Layout: Writte in two columns, 260 x 175–180 (column 80–85) mm, 44–45 lines.

Binding: Modern carton binding.

History: Previously belonged probably to Jacques Bongars (1554–1612). The manuscript is however not mentioned in Samuel Hortin's Clavis bibliothecae Bongarsianae from 1634. — Gift to the Bern Burgerbibliothek by Jakob Graviseth 1632.
