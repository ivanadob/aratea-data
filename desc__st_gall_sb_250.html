Parchment — 645 pp. — St. Gall — s. IX4/4

Collation: The quire structure can be examined to a certain extent in the digital facsimile thanks to the quire marks in Roman numerals centered in the lower margin of the last page of each quire. Uncertain is the collation for the quires VI and VII (including mainly tables on p. 99-140), for which there are no quire marks and which seem to be irregular. flyleaf2 (pastedown + p. 1), I12 (pp. 3-26), II (pp. 27-46), III 10 (pp. 47-66), IIII8 (pp. 67-82), V8 (pp. 83-98), VI+VII12(-1) (pp. 99-120), VIII10 (pp. 121-140), VIIII-XIIII10 (p. 141-260), XV6 (p. 261-278), XVI-XVIII10 (p. 279-332), XIIII 8 (p. 333-348), XX-XXIII10 (p. 349-428), XXIIII8+1 (p. 429-446), XXV8 (p. 447-462), XXVI10-1 (p. 463-480, missing 1 fol. after p. 478; p. 463-464 single leaves), XXVII-XXVIII8 (p. 481-512), XXVIIII6 (p. 513-524), XXX8-1 (p. 525-538, missing 1 fol. after p. 526), XXXI10 (p. 539-558), XXXII8 (p. 559-578), XXXIII-XXXV10 (p. 579-638), XXXVI2 (2 single leaves, p. 639-642), XXXVII2 (Bifolium, p. 643-645) .
