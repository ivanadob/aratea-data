Berlin, Staatsbibliothek, Ms. Phill. 1830
Facsimile
Show TEI-XML

Parchment — iii (paper) + 12 fols. + iii (paper) —

Collation: Fols. 1-2 are a bifolium (dimensions 233x285 mm); Fols. 3-10 is a regular quarto (size 235x300 mm); Fols. 11-12 are bifolium (size 214-224 x 296-300 mm); Quiremark 'I' (2v). To judge by the note on f. 1v, the current fols. 3-10 with the easter tables were originally placed within the bifolium fols. 1-2, forming one quire. Fols. 11-12 was probably part of Germanicus' Aratea, now preserved as Berlin, Staatsbibliothek, Ms. Phill. 1832 (see there for history of both manuscripts).

Selected bibliography:

1r Blank.

1v-2v Dionysius Exiguus: Argumenta Paschalia ›Incipit liber dyonisii exigui‹

  • Preface Domino beatissimo et nimium desiderantissimo patri petrono episcopo … — … diuina gratia custodire dignetur. ›Explicit prefatio.‹ A contemporary note at the lower boarder of f. 1v > hoc signum require in fine cicli guided the reader to find the rest of the text after the paschal tables. The text follows now immediately, suggesting that the leaves with the tables were moved.
  • (2v) ›Incipit cyclus decennouenalis quem greci ennea cai decaderida uocant‹ Contemporary note on the included tables: > Constitutus a sanctis patribus in quo XIIImas lunas paschales … decimus lunaris est. Hic autem cyclus dynosii quinque decennovenalibus constat hoc est XCV annis. Sumitque exordium a DXXII anno incarnationis domini et desini in DCXXVI anno. Hunc ergo cyclum pretermisimmus, eo quod totus praeteriit.

On the measures of time Annus solaris habet IIIIor tempora … — … Unum momentum habet ostentum unum et dimidium.

Argumentum for the leap year ›Argumentum unde creatum sit bissextum‹ Annus habet horas VIII DCC has partire per VII … — … Sex palmae faciunt cubitum.
