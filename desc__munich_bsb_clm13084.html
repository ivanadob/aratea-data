Part I (ff. 1-47)

Freising — s. IXin

History: According to Costrino the text of the "Disputatio" was copied in Freising from Munich, Bayerische Staatsbibliothek, Clm 6407 (itself written in Verona during the time of Pacificus and sent early in the s. IX to Freising).

1r-26v Alcuin: Disputatio de rhetorica Diagrams at the end of the text.

26v-47r Alcuin: Dialogus de dialectica Digram of the division of science at the end of the text (f. 47r).
