Miscellany on music, geometry and astronomy

Aratea text: Hyginus, De astronomia

Parchment — 170 fols. — 345 × 262 mm — Southern Germany or Austria — s. XII2

Support: Quiremarks on the first recto of some quires: II (12r), III (20r), V (36r), VIIII (70r), X (73r), XII (90r), XIII (92r), XIIII (100r), XXIIII (162r), written in red or later black ink; most of the texts have running titles, naming the author and/or work; the geometrical and astronomical sections have running titles 'Geometria' and 'Astronomia'.The manuscripts is foliated 1-166 including four slips: 16a, 42a, 76a, 91a; and two folia skipped by the previous folliation 45a, 66a.

Layout: Written mostly in two columns, around 47 lines. The illustrations are discussed in Hermann.

Selected bibliography:Saxl, see in Archive reference. pp. 69-75. — Blume, Dieter, Mechthild Haffner, and Wolfgang Metzger. Sternbilder des Mittelalters. Vol. I, 1: Text und Katalog der Handschriften. Berlin: Akademie Verlag, 2012, pp. 536-540. — Hermann, H. J. Die illuminierte Handschriften und Inkunabeln der Nationalbibliothek in Wien. Vol. 2: Die deutschen romanischen Handschriften (Leipzig, 1926), pp. 261-270. — The Musica of Hermannus Contractus. Translated and edited by Leonard Ellinwood. Revised with a new introduction by John L. Snyder. University of Rochester Press,2015, pp. 47-51.
