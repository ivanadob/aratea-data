Bern, Burgerbibliothek, Cod. 45
Facsimile

Lucanus: De bello civili; Dracontius: Orestes; Hyginus: De astronomia; Figurae Graecorum Hyginus, De astronomia

Parchment — Fleury — s. IX2/3(Bischoff 1998, Munk Olsen 1982–89); s. IX2 (Homburger 1962)

Parchment I5 (ff.1-9, wants 10), II-VI4 (ff.10-49), VII5+1 (ff.50-60, f. 60 added on a stub), with quiremarks partially preserved (II on f. 17v and Q V on f. 33v). Carolingian minuscule from Fleury (s. Homburgeg and Mittenhuber).

Modern binding.

History: The manuscripts was probably in Fleury - a manuscript with the title "Lucani bellum civile" is listed in the catalogue from 1552. Belonged to Pierre Daniel (1530-1603), Jacques Bongars and Jakob Graviseth (see exlibris from all three on f. 1r); the latter donated it to the Burgerbibliothek in Bern.
