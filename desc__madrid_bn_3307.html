Astronomical-computistical collection

Aratea text: De ordine ac positione; Excerptum de astrologia

Parchment — 71 fols. — 302 × 245 mm — Murbach (Borst) or Metz — 826-850

The manuscript represents a fragmentary copy of the Aachen encyclopedia (Lib. comp.). It must have been a luxury copy made perhaps for a member of the royal family (Borst, Schriften (Vol. I, pp. 248-249) suggested that the exemplar came from Aachen). The Excerptum de astrologia and the star catalogue De ordine ac positione, open the fifth book of the encyclopedia.

Collation:

Layout: Written in one column, 47 lines per page.
