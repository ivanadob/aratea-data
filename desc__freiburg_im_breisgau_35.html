Aratea text: De signis caeli

Parchment — — Lotharingia — ca. 850

Collation:

Binding: Bound with two printed works: Bede, De natura rerum (Basle: H. Petri, 1529) and Beatus Rhenanus, In C. Plinium (Basle: J. Froben, 1526).

Selected bibliography:Blume, Dieter, Mechthild Haffner, and Wolfgang Metzger. Sternbilder des Mittelalters. Vol. I, 1: Text und Katalog der Handschriften. Berlin: Akademie Verlag, 2012, pp. 249-255. — Hagenmaier, W. Kataloge der Universitätsbibliothek Freiburg i.B., v. 1, pt. 4 (Wiesbaden, 1988). — Lippincott, K. Entry in Saxl Project.

1r-11r De signis coeli Illustrations of 34 constellations. For detailed description and analysis see Blume and Lippincott.
