Scholia Strozziana

Introduction by Ivana Dobcheva

Introduction

Illustrated are only MSS Vatican, BAV, Urb. lat. 1358 and Madrid, BN, 19. In MSS Palermo, Biblioteca Comunale, 2 Qq. E. 11 and Vatican, BAV, Reg. lat. 1801 there are blank places left for the illustrations.
